ABOUT US

MISS U INC., is a tech company based in Pasig City, established in 2023 by a group of innovative individuals all students from the Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Pasig under the course Bachelor of Science in Information Technology section 1B, MISS U Inc. represents a collective passion for originality and inventiveness in the realm of web design. Our committed team's goal is to become masters at creating visually stunning and intuitively functional websites. We bring our creative capabilities to every project, from conception to completion. We at MISS U INC. are dedicated to breaking down barriers, using our creative abilities, and producing amazing, one-of-a-kind creations. Join us at MISS U Inc., where we are constantly reinventing web design, one creative project at a time, with a foundation based on knowledge, passion, and ingenuity.
